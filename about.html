Our Story

Rooted in Gujarat,
Serving the World

Ridhee Agritech is a professionally managed agro-processing & dehydration unit based in Anjar-Kachchh, Gujarat. We specialise in high-quality dehydrated fruits, vegetables, herbs and customised food ingredients for domestic and global markets.

Who We Are

Built on Purity
& Precision

We combine advanced hot-air and low-temperature dehydration technology with strict hygiene standards and consistent sourcing to deliver reliable, natural, shelf-stable products that meet the needs of the modern food industry.

From cleaning and grading to pulverising, milling, blending and customised formulations — every step of our process is designed to preserve the natural goodness of the raw ingredient without any additives or preservatives.

  • Advanced hot-air & low temperature dehydration technology
  • Controlled moisture & microbial standards for every batch
  • Custom cuts, grades, mesh sizes & private-label supply
  • Bulk, OEM & export-ready packaging options
  • Traceable sourcing from approved farms

Our Capabilities

Multi-Product Agro Processing

◈

Cleaning & Grading

Every raw material goes through a rigorous multi-stage cleaning process — washing, air-cleaning and manual inspection — before being graded by size, colour and quality. Only the best-grade produce moves forward to processing, ensuring each batch meets our strict standards for purity, consistency and buyer specifications.

♨

Drying & Dehydration

We use advanced hot-air and low-temperature dehydration technology to gently remove moisture from fresh fruits, vegetables and herbs. Our process preserves natural colour, aroma and nutritional value while achieving a consistent moisture level of 4–8%, ensuring a shelf life of up to 18 months without any preservatives.

❋

Pulverising & Milling

Our in-house milling facility produces powders in a wide mesh range of 50 to 300 mesh, fully customisable to buyer requirements. Whether you need a coarse granule for seasoning blends or an ultra-fine powder for nutraceutical capsules, we calibrate our pulverising equipment precisely to match your formulation needs.
