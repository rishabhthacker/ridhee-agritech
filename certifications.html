Certifications

Our Compliance Framework

FSSAI Licensed Facility

Our processing unit operates under a valid FSSAI license, ensuring full compliance with India's Food Safety & Standards Authority regulations for domestic and export supply.

GMP Compliant Practices

Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) guidelines are rigorously followed throughout our facility — from raw material intake to final packaging — ensuring product integrity.

MSME Registered

Registered under the Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSME), Government of India — validating our standing as a certified domestic manufacturer and exporter.

Microbial Testing

Every batch undergoes microbial testing to verify safety parameters including TPC, coliform, yeast & mould counts — before dispatch.

Pesticide Testing

Products are tested for pesticide residues to meet domestic and international standards, ensuring safe, clean ingredients for all end-use applications.

Traceable Sourcing

Raw materials are procured from approved farms with documented sourcing trails, enabling full traceability from field to finished product for every batch.
